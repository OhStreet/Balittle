Welcome to Balittle!

This is a small-scale take on the hit game "Balatro" by LocalThunk, written by me for the Unit 1 project.

The Rules:

You have one round to play, with the goal of beating the 'blind'.

When the round starts, 8 playing cards are drawn. You must select up to 5 cards from your draw to form and play poker hands.

Each playing card has a base 'chip' value.
For instance, cards with numerical ranks (2 of spades, 5 of hearts, 10 of clubs) are worth chips according to their rank (2, 5, 10 respectively). Face cards are worth 10 chips, and Aces are worth 11.

Depending on the poker hand played, the total chip value of the hand will be multiplied by a set value:

High Card: x1 - Default case if there are no valid poker hands. Picks the card worth the most chips.
Pair: x2 - Two cards of the same rank (number).
Two Pair: x3 - Two sets of pairs played together.
Three of a Kind: x3 - Three cards of the same rank.
Flush: x5 - Five cards of the same suit (hearts, spades, clubs, diamonds).
Straight: x6 - Five cards played in consecutive order (Ex: 10, 9, 8, 7, 6 or A, K, Q, J, 10).
Full House: x7 - Five cards made up of a Three of a Kind and a Pair.

Before the round begins, you get to pick 3 tarot cards out of 6 randomly drawn cards. These cards act as passives that will alter how your deck functions, such as adding chips to cards or increasing multiplier.

You get 5 hands to play in the round, and 3 free shuffles. If you manage to beat the target score before running out of hands, you win!

Fool
Randomizes chip values of all selected cards (1 - 20)
Magician
Doubles the chip value of all selected cards.
Priestess
Increases hand multiplier by 3.
Empress
Adds 5 chips to each selected card.
Emperor
Doubles the hand multiplier.
Hierophant
Adds 5 chips to every card with the hearts suit.
Chariot
Adds 30 chips to the highest value card in hand.
Strength
Adds 2 chips to each selected card and increases hand multiplier by 1.
Hermit
If only one card is played, triples its chip value and increases hand multiplier by 1.
Justice
The lowest value card in hand receives a 20-chip bonus.
Temperance
Reduces hand multiplier by 1 (minimum 1), but adds 10 chips to each selected card.
Judgement
Triples chips of every card in hand but reduces hand multiplier by 2 (minimum 1).
